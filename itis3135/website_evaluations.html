Zainab Bello || ITIS 3135 Website Evaluations

Bad Website Example
  • Contrast: The abundance of colors on this website hinders visibility, making it difficult to discern, and the elements fail to stand out effectively.
  • Repitition: The absence of uniformity on this website poses a challenge for readers in distinguishing between the title and the subtitle.
  • Alignment: The website appears disorganized, making it unappealing.
  • Proximity: This website lacks space to group similar elements together, making it hard for readers to understand the website contents
Good Website Example
  • Contrast: The visibilty of the elements on this website stands out and easy to read.
  • Repitition: The repetition throughout the entire website creates a sense of consistency.
  • Alignment: All elements on the website are visually connected, resulting in a well-organized appearance.
  • Proximity: Related elements are effectively grouped together, while those that are unrelated have enough space and a distinct design.
