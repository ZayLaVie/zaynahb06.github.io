Zainab Bello's Hobby:
Crocheting

Crocheting is a versatile and creative craft that involves using a hooked needle to interlock yarn or thread into intricate patterns. The rhythmic motion of crocheting can be both meditative and rewarding, allowing crafters to create a wide range of items, from cozy blankets and stylish garments to delicate lacework. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced crafter, crocheting offers a fulfilling way to express your artistic flair and produce handmade treasures.

How

To crochet, start by selecting a suitable yarn and crochet hook size based on the project you have in mind. Begin with a slipknot and place it on the hook, then proceed to make a foundation chain of desired length. Once the foundation chain is ready, engage in various crochet stitches like single crochet, double crochet, or others, depending on your pattern. Continue following the pattern instructions, creating rows or rounds until you achieve the desired size and shape for your project.
